Scientific Methodology

Applied Scientific Framework λ Development Model

01

Strategic Diagnosis

A structural analysis of the organisational environment is performed to identify critical variables, systemic constraints, and strategic opportunities, formulating digital improvement hypotheses based on evidence.

02

Data Collection and Modelling

Quantitative and qualitative data are collected and structured in order to model the functional and technological architecture of the proposed digital system.

03

Analysis and Experimental Design

A preliminary design is developed based on principles of usability, user experience, and validation of strategic hypotheses through functional prototypes.

04

Engineering and Technical Structuring

The technical structure is built using software engineering principles, ensuring scalability, modularity, and operational efficiency.

05

Controlled Development and Validation

Development is executed under quality standards, with functional testing, performance validation, and version control.

06

Implementation and Post-Launch Evaluation

After implementation, monitoring is carried out through performance indicators (KPIs) to evaluate results and continuously optimize the system.
