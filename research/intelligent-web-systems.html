Elysium λ Development & Research — Portugal, European Union

Abstract

Keywords: intelligent web systems, SME digitalisation, adaptive interfaces, automated decision support, applied AI, human-computer interaction.

III.

Research Questions

The following research questions guide this pilot study:

RQ1

To what extent can adaptive, AI-augmented web interfaces improve task completion efficiency and user satisfaction among non-specialist SME personnel relative to conventional static web systems?

RQ2

What minimum data requirements and architectural configurations are sufficient to enable meaningful automated decision-support functionality within resource-constrained SME web environments?

RQ3

How do SME operators perceive the transparency, trustworthiness, and interpretability of AI-generated outputs integrated into their operational web platforms?
