Moyra

History — Food Studio & Takeaway

← Back to Portfolio

Moyra defines itself as a Food Studio and Takeaway establishment. It is fascinating how they intentionally distance themselves from the "fast food" concept, aiming to position themselves in a superior, more exclusive, and artisanal category.

Location: Their operations centre is located in La Aurora, in the province of Heredia, Costa Rica.

Gastronomic Offer & Services

Variety: Their menu covers both sweet and savoury options, allowing them to cater for different meal times: breakfasts, lunches, desserts, or afternoon coffee.

Convenience as a Central Axis: The primary focus is on offering practical solutions. As "ready-to-go" food or through online orders, they aim to simplify the client's life by delivering a high-quality finished product without the need for the consumer to spend time in the kitchen.

Diversification

In addition to daily retail sales, they have the capacity to handle custom orders and events, suggesting they offer catering or larger-scale production services for special occasions.

"Artisan and Premium: The brand emphasises attention to detail, the use of fresh ingredients, and the implementation of their own recipes. Their promise is to deliver a 'premium experience' designed to be enjoyed in the comfort of home."

National Identity

They proudly highlight being a 100% Costa Rican project and carry the "Hecho en CR" (Made in CR) seal, which strongly appeals to support for local entrepreneurship and national identity.

Communication Tone

This is a highly remarkable aspect. Although they sell a premium experience, the brand speaks to the client in a warm and close manner, using the characteristic "voseo" of the country. This creates a bond of trust and familiarity with the local consumer.

Channels & Digital Tools

The business is perfectly adapted to the dynamics of current digital commerce. They feature clear calls to action and integrate fundamental channels to facilitate the shopping experience:

Maps

Allowing clients to easily locate the Food Studio for order collection.

WhatsApp

An indispensable channel in Costa Rica for direct enquiries, personalised service, and order reception.

Online Menu

Enabling quick and easy self-management for the user.

Summary

Moyra presents a highly attractive business model aligned with current trends. They have successfully identified a consumer who has little time available but is demanding and unwilling to sacrifice quality, homemade flavour, and artisanal detail for immediacy.
