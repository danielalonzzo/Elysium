UX Architecture & Interface Analysis

About Me (Sobre Mim)

The goal: To establish authority and empathy simultaneously. The layout breaks away from traditional corporate grids by using asymmetrical framing and prominent typography.

We highlight his 20+ years of clinical experience right at the top, followed by a personal narrative that connects with the patient's anxieties before they even book an appointment.

Osteopathy Services

The goal: Demystifying the treatment. Patients often arrive with specific pain points, so the interface acts as a triage system.

We implemented clear, pill-shaped tags to immediately identify treatable symptoms. The visual hierarchy guides the user seamlessly towards the "Book Consultation" call to action, removing friction from the conversion funnel.

Patient Portal Integration

The goal: Transforming a static website into a functional operating system for the business.

The login and registration interface is designed with minimalism to reduce cognitive load. By securing this area behind authentication, patients can manage their appointments, history, and tailored exercise plans, creating a loop of retention and continuous care.
